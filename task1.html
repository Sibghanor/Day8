Tribute to Imran Khan

Introduction

Imran Khan, born on October 5, 1952, is a former cricketer and the 22nd Prime Minister of Pakistan. He is widely known for his leadership, both on the cricket field and in politics.

Goals

Imran Khan's primary goals as a leader include promoting justice, fighting corruption, and improving the socio-economic conditions in Pakistan. He has dedicated his life to creating a better and more just society.

Achievements

  • Led Pakistan to its first Cricket World Cup victory in 1992.
  • Founded Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital & Research Centre.
  • Established Namal University to provide quality education.
  • Elected as the Prime Minister of Pakistan in 2018.

Quote

"Compromise for your dream but never compromise on your dream."
